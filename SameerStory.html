Sameer's Diary

24th May 2020
Dear Diary,
The lockdown has already been extended for 60 days now. I resent sitting up here throughout the day - on this laptop and literally doing nothing but sitting alone. I feel terrible locked in here. Though mom clearly makes wonderful food, her experiments with them cuz I'm home only prove that she is awesome. But I clearly feel weird locked in here.

Father has sufficient savings, but he clearly is in distress. His employer has certainly cut his salary and I know for sure he is hiding stuff.
The bussiness is bad, the economy is bad and everything is bad except for the government which will have enough to digest and the academic institutions which will have enough to feign tutions and earn.
Seriously, diary, do you think all the people we trust are good people - like right from Prime Minister to our very family? I have come to suppose not. Some people like our parents are good to us, and not so good to probably their extended kin or maybe their employees or collegues. While some like the government are good only to those who shall elect them or at least to those who have enough sympathy from all peopleso that the government can only invest there and still have everyone's support.
This is a bloody place, I tell you.

Oh, do you know, Karthik contacted corona last week and is still in hospital, though the doctors say he is fine. He is a great chap, you know.
I just wish he gets well and this lockdown gets over too.
Good night, diary.

-Sameer,
Delhi
