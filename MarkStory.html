Mark's Letter
...A paper found in a church in Brazil...
23rd May 2020
It has been 2 days since I ate something good. It has been a month since I visited school and about a week since I've seen mom.
I don't know how mom would be doing anyway.
I do not remember mom sinning. She was such a pious lady.
Her hair was beautiful and so was her smile. She used to visit the Church every sunday, even though the local magistrate asked us not to.
She obeyed the president's call to work. She was a true patriot. por que ela deve sofrer?
Oh senhor! Why must we be punished in this way?

The president said there shall be no disease, there shall be no deaths and that everything shall be peaceful
Now, there is no food, there is no clean water and the President never comes to help.
I need help. And my mom needs help too. We are a 209 million strong people, and about 10,000 of us are already dead.
The man in the white mask who brings me food everyday is the only one I like now. Senhor, please come forth. Help me now.
Please return mãe. The padre says you are the only one who can do it. I believe him. That stupid doctor said, you shall now
let mãe sleep in her grave. I do not want that, Lord. Let her rest, but ask her to speak to me often.
I shall be here next Sunday too, Deus.
Please ask her to wake up soon.

- Mark
A week later, a news stormed the national news,
"...13-years old kid dies of the deadly wave of infections that have hit the globe. This has only strengthened protests against
the President's policies and down-playing of the risks involved in COVID-19. A heart-wrenching letter written by the boy was found in a local
church by our correspondents where he is asking god to ask his mother to wake up. The mother of the child passed away three weeks ago..."

You've earned Mark's souvenir
